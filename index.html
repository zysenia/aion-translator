Translate Elyos and Asmodian Faction Chat

Aion Translator is a lightweight tool designed to decode cross-faction chat in Aion Classic and Retail. It helps players make sense of scrambled messages sent between Elyos and Asmodians and also allows composing messages that are readable by the opposite faction.

With built-in support for in-game icons and a clean interface, this translator enhances communication, immersion, and coordination across factions. 100% client-side, ad-free, and fast.
